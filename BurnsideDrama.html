Drama Opportunities

The Drama department offer a variety of after-school activites covering different acting experiences. These include groups on Shakespeare, improvisation and play writing. These clubs are used to prepare participants for the annual school show which is presented in collaboration with the Music department. There are also several annual visits to local performances of plays and musicals.
